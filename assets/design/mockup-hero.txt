═════════════

• Cards: White background with subtle shadow and hover lift effect
• Typography: Poppins for headings, Inter for body text
• Spacing: 24px grid gap, 48px section margins
• Interaction: Smooth 0.3s transitions on hover
• Accessibility: WCAG AA compliant contrast ratios
